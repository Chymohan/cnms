College Notice Management System

A smart and reliable platform to manage, publish, and access college notices anytime, anywhere.

The College Notice Management System (CNMS) simplifies the way colleges share important announcements. It allows administrators and teachers to publish notices digitally, while students can easily view, search, and download notices from a single, organized platform—saving time and reducing dependency on physical notice boards.
